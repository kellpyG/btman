Welcome we are Bituman Aviation

Bituman Aviation Services Limited is an Aviation fuel supply firm based in Wilson Airport, Nairobi, Kenya and with clientele within Kenya. The firm was established in the year 2019 and has continued to grow at a commendable pace with a highly professional workforce. The firm provides a full range of products and services in the supply of Aviation fuel and petroleum products. The firm is specialized in supply for both Governmental and Non-Governmental Organisations.
