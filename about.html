About Us

Firm's Background

Bituman Aviation Services Limited is an Aviation fuel supply firm based in Wilson Airport, Nairobi, Kenya and with clientele within Kenya. The firm was established in the year 2019 and has continued to grow at a commendable pace with a highly professional workforce. The firm provides a full range of products and services in the supply of Aviation fuel and petroleum products. The firm is specialized in supply for both Governmental and Non-Governmental Organisations.

Mission Statement

The firm’s mission is “to provide our clients with relevant, timely and professional products and services”. In order to achieve this, we recognise the need to respond to our clients’ needs promptly and deliver the required products and services that vary considerably in a satisfactory manner. We endeavour to ensure that we live up to our mission by developing and retaining high calibre staff who are trained to identify and effectively assess clients’ needs as well as recommend the viable solutions and tools. Our good working relationship with the client is considered fundamentally important and the sustainability of the relationship is based in performance and responsive attitude we maintain. Our commitment to serve our clients beyond the minimum expectations and delivery of satisfactory products and services underlines our focus in professionalism

Our Approach

Our approach is dictated by the objective of the service as guided by predetermined standards and requirements. We carry our service interactively with the management and those appointed by the management while safeguarding our objectivity and independence. The value of the services we provide far outweighs the fees we charge in virtually all the cases. We endeavour to maintain quality services to our clients at affordable fees at all times.

Our clientele vary considerably and their nature of clients is however not limited to the above. We are able to provide professional services to different types of institutions
